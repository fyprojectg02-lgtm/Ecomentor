# EcoMentor Project: Feature Analysis

This document provides a comprehensive list of features for the Student and Teacher dashboards within the EcoMentor platform. The project is an AI-powered environmental education platform that gamifies sustainability through "EcoPoints" and real-world impact tracking.

## 🎓 Student Dashboard Features

The Student Dashboard is designed to engage learners through gamification, AI-driven personalization, and community interaction.

### 1. Dashboard & Analytics
- **Personalized Overview**: Dynamic greeting with a real-time summary of EcoPoints, active learning paths, completed tasks, and global rank.
- **Environmental Impact Estimator**: Visualizes real-world impact through metrics like **CO2 saved (kg)**, **Trees equivalent**, and **Plastic reduced (kg)**.
- **Eco-Points System**: Multi-layered point tracking (Total, Weekly, Monthly) with a visual status of pending submissions.

### 2. AI-Powered Learning & Assessment
- **AI Learning Path Generator**: Creates custom educational modules based on the student's education level (School/College) and specific interests.
- **Learning Modules**: Interactive, structured content with progress tracking and point rewards upon completion.
- **AI Quiz Generator**: Topic-specific quizzes (Climate Change, Biodiversity, etc.) generated by Gemini AI with adjustable difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard).
- **Answer Review & Explanations**: Immediate feedback after quizzes with AI-generated explanations for correct/incorrect answers.

### 3. Gamification & Engagement
- **Educational Games Hub**:
    - **Eco Quiz**: Knowledge-based challenges.
    - **Recycling Sorting**: Interactive waste management simulation.
    - **Carbon Track**: Real-world footprint tracking.
    - **Energy Saver**: Time-based energy efficiency game.
- **Community Leaderboard**: Global and classroom-specific rankings with trophies for top performers.
- **EcoBot Assistant**: Integrated AI chatbot (EcoBot) for instant guidance, task suggestions, and personalized learning support.

### 4. Real-World Action
- **Task Recommendations**: Personalized eco-tasks suggested by AI based on the student's level and progress.
- **Action Submission System**: Students can submit proof (photos/descriptions) of real-world environmental actions for teacher review.
- **NGO Opportunities**: A dedicated platform connecting students with volunteering and learning opportunities from NGOs.

## 👩‍🏫 Teacher Dashboard Features

The Teacher Dashboard provides educators with advanced tools for content creation, student management, and assessment.

### 1. Classroom Management & Analytics
- **Teacher Portal Overview**: Centralized view of total students, pending reviews, active lesson plans, and cumulative class points.
- **Student Performance Tracking**: A comprehensive analytics table showing individual student levels, EcoPoints, tasks completed, and specific environmental impact metrics.
- **Classroom Organization**: Tools to manage classrooms, subjects, academic years, and student enrollments.

### 2. AI-Driven Content Creation
- **AI Lesson Planner**: A sophisticated tool that generates complete lesson plans, including:
    - Learning objectives and duration.
    - Materials needed.
    - Classroom activities.
    - Assessment methods.
    - Differentiation strategies for different learners.
- **AI Rubric Generator**: Automatically creates detailed assessment rubrics for tasks, projects, or classroom participation.

### 3. Verification & Grading
- **Review Queue**: A streamlined system for teachers to review student eco-action submissions.
- **Verification Workflow**: Teachers can approve/reject submissions, award EcoPoints, and provide written feedback.
- **Flagging System**: Automated or manual flagging of submissions that require special attention.

### 4. Communication & Notifications
- **NGO Connect**: Allows teachers to discover or manage NGO opportunities for their students.
- **Notification Center**: Real-time alerts for flagged submissions, student milestones, and classroom activity.

## 🛠️ Core Infrastructure Features
- **Eco-Logic Points Engine**: Backend logic for calculating rankings, awarding points, and resetting periodic scores.
- **Impact Calculation Engine**: Scientific conversion of actions into environmental metrics (trees, water, CO2).
- **Multi-Level Education Support**: Tailored experience for both School and College students.
- **AI Integration Layer**: Seamless integration with Google Gemini AI for all generative features (quizzes, lessons, rubrics).
